Beyond the Backtest

Two analytical layers that deepen the research beyond standard performance comparison.

GARCH(1,1) Volatility Modelling

Standard rolling volatility treats each day equally. GARCH captures the empirical fact that volatility clusters — large moves beget large moves.

σ²ₜ = ω + α · ε²ₜ₋₁ + β · σ²ₜ₋₁

Fitted on all 7 strategy return series. Outputs: conditional volatility time series, 30-day forward VaR cones, and Ljung-Box ARCH effects test confirming clustering.

COVID peak conditional vol was 3–4× the 10-year average across all strategies
CVaR strategy showed lowest max conditional volatility by design
All 7 strategies show significant ARCH effects (Ljung-Box p < 0.05)
